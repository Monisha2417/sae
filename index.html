What is Baja sae India ?

Baja SAE India is an exciting annual off-road racing event organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) India. It challenges engineering students from colleges across the country to design, build, and race custom off-road vehicles. It is held annually every year at Pithampur, Indore.

Objective

The main objective of Baja SAE India is to provide engineering students with a challenging project that involves designing, fabricating, and testing a single-seater off-road vehicle that can withstand rough terrain, mud, water, and other challenging conditions.

Learning Opportunities

Participating in Baja SAE India offers students a hands-on learning experience in various aspects of vehicle design, fabrication, teamwork, project management, and problem-solving.It provides a platform for students to interact with professionals from the automotive industry, gain exposure to industry-standard practices, and potentially secure internships or job opportunities.

Competetion Format

  • Design Phase: Teams are required to submit a comprehensive design report detailing their vehicle's design, specifications, and engineering rationale.
  • Virtual Baja: This stage involves the evaluation of the submitted design reports by a panel of judges. Teams may also need to present their designs virtually and respond to queries from the judges.
  • Dynamic Events: Teams whose designs pass the virtual round proceed to the dynamic events, which include various tests such as acceleration, maneuverability, hill climb, suspension & traction, and endurance.
  • Static Events: These events include presentation and evaluation of marketing strategies, cost analysis, and design justification.
  • Final Evaluation: The overall performance of the teams throughout the competition determines the final rankings.
